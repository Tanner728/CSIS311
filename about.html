Purpose

This project was created as part of the CSIS 311 Server-Side Scripting course at MSUM. Our goal is to help students and curious minds explore the wonders of physics through interactive simulations that are both educational and engaging.

The simulation tools provided aim to bridge the gap between theoretical concepts and real-world dynamics, offering a more intuitive grasp of gravitational forces, motion, and energy.
